Aegro - Lead UX Designer

BRAZIL

May 2017

Jul 2018

As Aegro's first designer (and later, with the team growing to 5 people, first Lead Designer), I had a fast-growing farm management product to 1) help reshape and rework where needed, given that it was already a fully-functioning product when I arrived; and 2) grow according to a design vision that we couldn't really point our fingers to yet. While I worked at Aegro, it multiplied it's headcount threefold. Our user base growth gained traction, and our product expanded greatly. Our design research process spanned giving support to high-ticket clients, flying across the country for field visits, reading the occasional farm-management manual and calling long-time farmers to test interactive prototypes or conduct interviews. Mapping these insights into the inner-workings of our software gave way to fruitful discussions of heuristics and system design between designers and developers. Most of my work was done with human interaction, pen and paper, Productboard and Figma.
